Project Brief

Chef will primarily serve as a marketplace connecting Home Chefs with Working Families & Young Professionals.

I undertook this thesis project as part of my UX design certification from Springboard Institute. The premise was to either design a new app or to redesign any existing app closely following the data gathered from the target audience. During this process, I learned the entire spectrum of user experience research from doing cardslots for the information architecture and to conducting A&B tests to solve high level issues
