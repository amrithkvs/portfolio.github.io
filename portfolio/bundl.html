Project Brief

From fitness to productivity to mental health, the quantified self movement means thereâ€™s a ton of apps to help us measure our lives, keep track of habits, and help us improve

As part of Shopify's Design Challenge we were asked to imagine launching a new app that helps someone understand how their daily choices help or hinder their goals every week. The app would make it easy for someone to log their information every day in order to get a weekly progress report that helps them do even better in the weeks to come.

Concept

We were asked to design a single-page website to launch our life logging app to the world. What kind of data would it collect, and how? What habit does it help the user build? and What will keep them coming back, week after week?

To incorporate all the above requested information, I decided to create an app that tracks sleep patterns, fitness levels, nutrition, finance and the users mental state. The landing page was designed to incorporate all the above apps while not burdening the user with too much information

Identity

As the app combines five different trackers to create a bigger life log app, I decided to call it Bundle with a play on the spelling to boost search engine optimization

The bold letters are spaced in a way to inculcate a sense of confidence into the user while the pink and purple color scheme keeps the product warm enough for first time users

Helvetica Neue

Landing Page

As the design challenge asked very specific questions, I felt it could be added to the landing page as a sort of introduction to a first time user about the platform. The sections included are:

What is Bundl? This section displays the problem solving or oppurtunity providing statement for the users

Who you are This section covers the types of users who might be eventually interested to use the service

How it works This section covers links to description pages for each tracker available on the platform

Why Bundl? This section covers the unique value proposition offered to the target audience

Sleep Tracker

Getting eight hours of sleep is not only important for your physical health but also for your mental health. Research suggests, if a human being is not sleeping for a minimum of atleast 6 hours after an eight hour shift, the body tends to become sluggish and motivation to be productive becomes difficult. The Sleep tracker has additional features such as:

Smart Lights this feature will allow the user to connect any smart bulb on the market and have minimal control in the form of on/off from the platform

Smart Cooling This feature will allow the user to adjust their temperature control by connecting it to any of the numerous smart thermometers

Smart Alarm Once the house is fitted with an alarm system, the platform can connect to it and control the security of their home

Finance Tracker

The idea behind this screen is to give the user at a glance a complete snapshot of their current spending habits. The bar at the top shows a total for the month in terms of income and expenses. The next section covers day to day income and expenses by breaking them down into individual categories

Fitness Tracker

Through this tracker the user can anaylze their walking, running, cycling, weight lifting and more. Each section has the following sub sections: Goal meter, Duration of exercise, Calories burned, Average heart rate and Average speed.

Nutrition Tracker

The nutrition tracker was designed to have three main sections:

The first concentrated on the calorie intake perday displayed on a progress bar against a given daily goal. The second displays the break down of the above calories in the form of Proteins, Fat & Carbs. The last section further breaks down the daily calorie intake into respective meals. Users will have to manually input the contents of their meal for the various section to be complete

Mood Tracker

As any persons current mood cannot be scientifically quantified, I chose to keep the screen more interactive than the other trackers which were more snapshots of data. The dial has a predetermined set of mood names associated to each of the numbers which will change where the title Good is displayed.
