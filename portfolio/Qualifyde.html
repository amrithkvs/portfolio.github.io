Project Brief

For most companies, hiring managers, interviewers and candidates, the recruitment process is broken. It’s a big problem that’s estimated to cost tens of millions in the tech industry alone. Most companies just limp along and throw resources at this broken, inefficient process.

The Qualifyde platform connects hiring managers or recruiters with interviewers that have the skills and availability to ensure a quick and efficient interview process. Using independent Qualifyde interviewers provides hiring managers with complete, consistent, and impartial candidate feedback in days, not weeks.

Concept

The client approached me with a very minimal MVP design. They wanted a complete redesign and allowed me approach it in anyway I deemed fit

I decided to have the application perform similarly to the UX of a typical website. The banner was contructed to create a simple navigation while revolving the User Experience around the main Dashboard screen. The key was to keep the platform uniform for all three users - Hiring Managers, Qualifyde Approved Interviwers and Candidates. I ensured this by designing the different features in an almost identical manner so there were no discrepencies between the interfaces.

Multiple Accounts

The Qualifyde platform caters to three types of users. Each type has their own unique set of benefits which are outlined below: .

Hiring Managers - Outsourcing interview process gives hiring managers control over the recruiting process yielding more Qualifyde candidates, faster. Hiring managers can specifiy interview questions and interact directly with interviewers and let Qualifyde do the heavy lifting of finding a pool of Qualifyde Candidates

Interviewer - Qualifyde simplifies the interview process so that you can do what you do best, interview! Qualifyde connects interviewers to hiring managers and candidates, schedules interviews and formats the assements recevied from interviewers. Interviewers can either get paid for the time invested or choose to volunteer.

Candidates - Qualifyde gives candidates the oppurtunity to get screened by professionals, impartial interviewers and to differentiate themselves in the market place. Candidates also have the oppurtunity to participate in mock interviews getting neutral feedback that can give them confidence and an advantage in a live interview

Dashboard

The user experience is designed with this screen as the nerve centre. It controls the main use case for the platform while allowing easy navigation to the supplementary screens.

The graph shows the user what their history on the platform is over the past six months - this was designed so that the user does not have to navigate additional 'history' pages. The footer contains three sub section:

Scheduled Interviews: Once the interview has been arranged using our pool of interviewers the hiring manager can now easily find updates and keep track of the said interviews.

Scheduling in progress: This tab allows the user to keep track of candidates who are yet to be matched with an expert interviewer. It is also helps the users can navigate to see potential system generated matches for them to approve or decline.

Incomplete Interview Request A sort of drafts page where users can get seamlessly access incomplete interview requests. This is made possible by an auto save feature at every step while the request is getting processed by the hiring manager

Team Management

Apart from helping the hiring managers find candidates and schedule interviews for them, the platform also allows the managers to keep track of the rest of their recruitement team. They can have the profiles of their candidate with updates neatly organised in a central hub. the columns are listed below:

1) Status of interview.

2) Ratings about interviewers

3) Feedback on candidates from said interviewers

4) Profile links to active interviewers assigned to candidates

5) Interview recordings for future referrences

Interactive Search

The aim was to keep the task as simple as possible for the interviewer to pick a candidate from our pool of available prospective candidates.

I chose the filter approach because the target audience is typically accustomed to such a controlled search environment. Users do not like to be burdned with too many options. , They are concerned only with relevant ones that might help them make a decision and this case the interviewer will be looking for the most suitable candidate that he/she might get to interview, thus the filters revolve specifically around the technical aspects of the required job postings.

Calendar Assistant

The users can have access to the smart calendar built into the platform. The calendar syncs across all platforms and can then notify the user in real time regarding any conflicts with potential interviews dates or time
