Project Brief

The client approached me with an idea to monetize different aspects of the impending legalization of medical marijuana in Canada. The client had a professional grow house, vast network of government connections, properitory machinery & unique products for sale. He ultimately wanted to give his business a digital footprint.

Concept

The client was very detailed about the types of pages he wanted which included: introduction, products, blog. consulting and resources

I decided as there is no singular screen more important than the other, the user can have easy access to all of them via the top navigation. The lesser visited pages are laid on the top right so the user is not burdened dealing with it constantly. The rest of the design is structered according to each indvidual screen and the use case offered on it

Identity

The logo was already designed by the company. I used their .png file after asking permission from the team in Toronto.

Colors

The client initially requested the traditional green color to match the color of the plant but I felt it was important to establish the colors scheme as a unique pallete by not following the herd. The result was a contrasting orange, blue and white with tints of grey/charcoal for the buttons

Products

This page was selected as the first screen after entering from the landing page because the marketing for the company revolves around these products so there is a high chance the users have landed on the website looking for these items. The client wanted to have a easy to use filter system that they can update when needed. The products they chose to have displayed were all uniqely sourced and thus needed a detailed description page with a cart call to action .

Patient Licensing

The second major usecase for the platform is for the community to apply for a medical marijuana license. The connections the client has facilitates these memberships at an expedited rate which is beneficial for patients facing chronic illness or pain. The registration form has the following fields to complete:

Applicant Information - First & Last name, Email address and Phone number

Contact Address - After a physical address has been inputed, users can select a preferred time to be contacted in this section

Health Care Practioner Information - This is to ensure the patient truly has the medical ailment they claim

Medical Documents/Health Card Upload - This helps to verify the users personal information. No application will go forward without an upload of either of the mentioned above documents

Blog

The client wanted a screen where they could share or write articles to share to their users. The interface design has three main sections:

learn more action for users to read further about the blog. The count is at 140 characters so the user is not reading multiple lines

social media sharing so the user can promote an article that resonates with them to their network

The blog footer gives snapshot information regarding of date & time of publishment and author name

Consultation Services

The client has multiple contacts across every vertical of this growing industry thus was interested and offering these connection as a service. The patient resources consist of four section:

Grow houses client will offer support for design, construction and finally to grow the plant within the facility.

Cannabis/Hemp this section covers information for first time users who do not understand the effects or benefits of using this plant.

Website Design As the industry is about to be open to the general public, the competition will start to pile up. To combat the impeding competition, they want to offer web design at a very competitive price

Investment Client envisioned a system where foreign investors can use his platform to make investments towards the growing cannabis industry. He was also hoping for this screen as a source of angel investment after a round of heavy marketing

Patient Resources

This screen aggregates the different resources the client has planned to offer. The spectrum of resources caters from first time to heavy long time users. Some of the unique resources available are:

Medical Cannabis - Geared towards first time users looking to find information about the side effects and benefits of marijuana use.

Licensed Producers - The client has partnered with numerous grow houses in the northern regions of Canada. These connections can be used to facilitate partnerships for upcoming establishments in the industry

ACMPR program - The client would like to facilitate access to cannabis for medical purposes regulation in canada. The program offers MMAR licenses to users who might benefit from using the herb

About Us

The client wanted a very comprehensive about us section which included links to the following sub sections:

Who we are - Intro section

What we do - Dwells into the macroeconomic details of the budding industry

How we are unique - Talks about the value proposition for users to do business with this platform and not the competition

Services - Break down of exact services offered by the platform both online and offline

#Endthestigma - Twitter hashtag to encourage the participation of the community to speed the legalisation process

My Account

This section will initially require a log in before the user have access to view or edit the following information:

Personal Details - First & last name, email address and phone number

Contact Address - Simple form including street address, city, state, country and zip code to fill

Payment Information - The platform will use this information while making purchases from the product section. If the user is not signed up, they will have to manually enter the payment information during checkout everytime they finalise a product

Order History - In regards to purchases, registered users can see their invoice #, status (open/close), date of purchase, payment status (paid/not paid), shipping tracker and the total amount paid.
